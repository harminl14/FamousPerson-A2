Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam - The Missile Man of India

Biography

"Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam was an aerospace scientist and the 11th President of India. He played a key role in India's missile and nuclear programs."

Achievements

  • Led India's missile development program (Prithvi & Agni).
  • Key role in India's 1998 nuclear tests (Pokhran-II).
  • Became the 11th President of India (2002-2007).
  • Received Bharat Ratna, India's highest civilian award.

Famous Quotes

"Dream, dream, dream. Dreams transform into thoughts and thoughts result in action."
